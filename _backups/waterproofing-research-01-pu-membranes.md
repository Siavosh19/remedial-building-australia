# Research — Liquid-applied PU membranes (category 1)  [AU sources; CONFIRM = not on AU source]

KEY CORRECTIONS vs current cards: WPM 157 is 1C MOISTURE-CURED (not "water-based");
WPM 155 Rapid Plus is a water-based PU-ACRYLIC HYBRID (not "Pure-PU"); Sikalastic-487 is
SOLVENT-BASED moisture-cured (VOC 180 g/L); Nitoproof 750 = moisture-cured, 810 = water-based.

1. ARDEX WPM 157 — 1C moisture-cured PU. AS4858 + AS4654. DFT 1.2mm. 1.04 kg/m² (~9–10 m²/20kg).
   Recoat 4–6h. Return-to-service 24h. Primer WPM 300 or WPM 270 (mandatory). Grey. 20kg pail (15.4L).
   Class CONFIRM. src: ardexaustralia.com/product/ardex-wpm-157/
2. ARDEX WPM 155 Rapid Plus — water-based PU-acrylic HYBRID. Class III AS4858 + AS4654. Coverage
   4L→4.6m², 15L→17.5m² (~1.1 m²/L). Drying 4h@23°C. STB tape (no bond breaker). Packs 5.3kg(~4L),
   20kg(~15L). Internal+external, tileable. Primer CONFIRM. src: ardexaustralia.com/product/ardex-wpm-155-rapid-plus/
3. ARDEX WPM 130 Builders Express — water-based modified PU-acrylic. Class III AS4858. Apply 1.0mm.
   Tileable within 4h. Coverage/primer CONFIRM. src: ardexaustralia.com/product/ardex-wpm-130/
4. Sika Sikalastic-487 — 1C SOLVENT-BASED moisture-curing PU. Class III >300% AS4858, AS4654.1.
   Tensile >2MPa. 2 coats, DFT 1.2mm, 680µm WFT/coat. Recoat "after one night, within 2 days".
   Full cure 24h; light traffic/screed after 24h. Primer: Sikalastic PU Primer / Moisture Seal /
   diluted 487. Substrate ≤5% moisture. Brush/roller. +5 to +35°C. Under-screed wet areas/balconies/
   podiums/decks, NON-UV. High stagnant-water resistance. VOC 180 g/L. 15L. Grey. Shelf 9 months.
   src: aus.sika.com sikalastic-487.html
5. SikaTile-110 Secure Proof — Class III, fibre-reinforced, RTU, water-based PU. AS4654:2012 + AS4858:2004.
   DFT min 1.0mm, min 2 coats, 0.75mm WFT/coat. 15L→~10m². Walls+floors, internal+external, under tile/stone.
   Grey. src: aus.sika.com sikatile-110-secureproof.html
6. Davco K10 Plus — water-based PU, Class III, premixed RTU. Under tile/stone; interior+exterior wet areas/
   terraces/balconies/roof/walkways. Coats 1–2h apart at 90°. Min dried 1.0mm. 6–8h dry before tiling.
   src: aus.sika.com davco-k10-plus.pdf
7. Bostik Dampfix PU — 1C highly elastic Class III PU, no bitumen, AS4858. 2-coat over primed porous/
